About



Ruchica Sinha is an experienced core committee member for the Developers Student Club (DSC), an Academic Support Volunteer at Make a Difference (MAD), and the Head of Media at Computer Society of India (CSI). She is currently pursuing a Bachelors of Technology - BTech degree in the field of Computer Science from Vellore Institute of Technology. In addition, she is also working as a freelance designer on multiple outsourced projects and building her own platform to create and sell her art.


Ruchica is skilled in Computer Science, Web Development, Event Management, Fine Arts, C++, Java, Digital and Graphic Design, Public Speaking, Teamwork, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, and Photoshop. She is a certified young journalist from Ryan TV, and has 2 publications of her artwork in the Polo India Calendar for the year 2018 and 2019. Ruchica has also had 3 art exhibitions in and around Bangalore, showcasing and selling her artwork to people from all around!
